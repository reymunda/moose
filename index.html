What is Moose

Moose is a parental control application that can help parents to monitor their children when using the internet. So, parents can keep their children are safe.

Content Monitor

We block dangerous content for kids. So, your kids can use internet safe. We send alert to you if your kids try to access.

Track Location

Track your kids with their gadget. It can help you to find where your kids. Also you can view the maps.

Time Schedule

You can set the schedule how long your kids can use their gadget and when they allowed to use their gadget.

We Monitor 20+ Apps

We are monitoring 20+ popular apps that can help parents get app usage information. So, their kids can get full control for safety.
